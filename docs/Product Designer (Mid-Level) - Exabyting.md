## About Exabyting

Exabyting is a technology and services company based in Dhaka, working with local and international clients to design, build, and scale digital products. Our work spans software development, product engineering, quality assurance, and UX design, supporting businesses across multiple industries.

We operate with small, cross-functional teams where designers, engineers, and product stakeholders work closely from problem definition to delivery. We value clear communication, practical decision-making, and taking responsibility for the quality of what we ship.

At Exabyting, we focus on sustainable work practices rather than hype. We invest in our people, expect ownership within defined scopes, and provide guidance and support so teams can do good work consistently and grow over time.

## What You’ll Do

* Understand client briefs and work closely with senior designers or product leads to clarify requirements and constraints  

* Translate requirements into user flows, wireframes, and UI designs with guidance and regular feedback  

* Design high-fidelity, responsive interfaces in Figma using components, auto-layout, and established design patterns  

* Apply and follow existing design systems; contribute incremental improvements when appropriate  

* Present design work, explain design rationale, and iterate based on feedback from senior designers and stakeholders  

* Create functional prototypes in Figma; experience with advanced or AI-assisted prototyping tools is a plus  

* Prepare clean, production-ready design handoffs and collaborate with developers during implementation  

* Participate in design QA to help ensure designs are shipped accurately and as intended  

This role is designed for designers who are responsible for execution, while receiving direction, feedback, and mentorship from senior team members.

## Requirements

* 2–4 years of professional experience as a Product, UX, or UI Designer  

* A portfolio demonstrating clear problem-solving, thoughtful UI decisions, and end-to-end execution  

* Hands-on experience working with existing design systems and reusable components  

* Foundational understanding of user research and usability testing principles (hands-on research experience is a plus, not a requirement)

* Willingness to learn, seek feedback, and continuously improve skills and ways of working
